# Job History Entry Template

Use this format when appending jobs to `job-history.md`:

```markdown
## [YYYY-MM-DD] - Search: "[search terms]"

| Job Title | Company | Location | Salary | Link | Fit | Notes |
|-----------|---------|----------|--------|------|-----|-------|
| [title] | [company] | [location] | [salary or N/A] | [url] | [High/Medium/Low/Skip] | [brief note] |
```

## Fit Score Criteria

- **High**: Matches target role, meets salary minimum, location works, no dealbreakers
- **Medium**: Partial match - missing 1-2 criteria but still worth considering
- **Low**: Significant gaps but not a dealbreaker
- **Skip**: Has a dealbreaker (note which one)

## Notes Format

Keep notes brief and actionable:
- "Strong match - [key reasons]"
- "Comp below target"
- "Agency - dealbreaker"
- "Crypto - dealbreaker"
- "Onsite only"
- "Requires [X] - dealbreaker"
